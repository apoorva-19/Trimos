Height Gauge V7


The height gauges V7 combine technological innovation and tradition. With their touch-display and lateral insert holders, which have proven their worth for decades, the V7 rank as universal instruments for the workshop.

The V7 are equipped with the revolutionary displacement handwheel allowing the user to choose either the manual or motorized displacement mode.
